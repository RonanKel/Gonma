Hello and welcome to a Gonma playtest.

This is a fishing rhythm game where you play creatures their songs to lift the curse an evil wizard fish has placed on them.
Make friends of the forest and fill out your journal!

Controls:
ESCAPE - Pause
SPACE - Cast your line / Reel in the creature once they bite / Scroll though dialog
Left click - Use to click buttons / Reel in the creature once they bite

Q - Used to "hit" the YELLOW notes when they pass over the note outline
W - Used to "hit" the CYAN notes when they pass over the note outline
E - Used to "hit" the MAGENTA notes when they pass over the note outline

For the purpose of this playtest, please try to talk through your thoughts. 
Vocalize anything you might be thinking, this will help us make the game better in the future!
